01 — Overview

One platform, three roles, zero paperwork.

The system brings students, teachers and administrators onto a single digital platform — replacing scattered spreadsheets and physical registers with structured courses, tracked progress and automated fee collection.

What It Solves

Institutions running classes across WhatsApp groups, paper registers and manual fee collection lose visibility fast. This LMS centralizes enrollment, content delivery, scheduling and payments into one accountable system — accessible from anywhere.

Core Objectives

  • Deliver a digital learning environment accessible from anywhere
  • Organize course content into a clear, structured hierarchy
  • Automate fee tracking and attendance instead of manual entry
  • Provide performance analytics for students and teachers alike

03 — User Roles

Every role sees exactly what it needs.

Permissions and dashboards are scoped per role, so no one wades through features that aren't theirs.

01
Student
Enrolls in courses, tracks progress, submits assignments, pays fees and views results in one place.
02
Teacher
Builds courses, uploads content, schedules classes and evaluates student performance.
03
Admin
Controls the entire system — user management, course approvals, reports and settings.
04
Parent
Optional role for monitoring a student's progress and fee status remotely.
04 — Feature Set

Every module a real course needs.

From chapters and lessons to fee automation, each module was built to work independently and together.

Course Management
Create courses with pricing, categories and prerequisites — with a full draft, published and archived workflow reviewed by admins.
Chapters & Structure
Sequential, lockable chapters with drag-drop reordering and estimated completion times per unit.
Lessons & Pages
Rich-text lessons, embedded content and quizzes, each with individual completion tracking.
Video Lessons
Adaptive HD streaming with speed control, subtitles, bookmarks and encrypted playback to prevent downloads.
PDF Resources
Built-in viewer with zoom, search and annotation for lecture notes and reference material.
Downloadable Files
Multi-format file support with size limits, virus scanning and download tracking.
Class Scheduling
Google Calendar sync, Zoom/Meet links and conflict detection on personalized timetables.
Fee Management
One-time, monthly or per-course billing with invoices, late fees, discounts and auto-reminders.
Results Management
Automated MCQ grading, weighted gradebooks and auto-generated transcripts and certificates.
05 — Panels

Three panels. One consistent system.

StudentPanel
Browse and enroll in courses, track learning progress, submit assignments, join discussion forums, message teachers directly, and download certificates on completion.
TeacherPanel
A drag-drop course builder, student performance analytics, assignment creation with plagiarism checks, live class hosting, announcements, and an earnings dashboard.
AdminPanel
Full user management, course approval workflows, payment gateway configuration, backups, audit logs, and exportable analytics dashboards.
06 — System Workflow

From sign-up to certificate, in nine steps.

A real sequence, not a marketing timeline — this is the actual path content and payments take through the system.

1
Registration & Verification
User signs up and verifies their email or phone via OTP.
2
Admin Approval
Teacher accounts are reviewed and approved by an administrator.
3
Course Creation
Teachers build course content, chapters and lessons.
4
Course Approval
Admin reviews and publishes the course.
5
Enrollment & Payment
Students enroll and complete payment through the fee module.
6
Content Consumption
Students move through lessons with progress tracked automatically.
7
Assignments & Grading
Submissions are collected and graded, automatically or manually.
8
Certificate Generation
Certificates and transcripts are issued on course completion.
9
Feedback & Ratings
Students rate the course, closing the loop for future improvements.
